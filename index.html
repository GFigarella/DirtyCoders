INSTRUCTIONS:


Welcome to the Rain or Shine Web App. To use this application, it's quite simple. Enter in the location in the appropriate field of your current location or your destination, and click the search button. Upon clicking the search button, the location will appear on the map and display the current weather conditions below the map.


Above the How To tab, there is two tabs displaying "indoor" and "outdoor." Here at Rain or Shine, we want to make sure you have a good day regardless of the weather conditions. Click on the indoor tab to display indoor activities in the location that you searched. Click on the outdoor tab to display outdoor activites in the location you searched.
